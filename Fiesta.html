The Street Dancing Competition in Macabalan, Cagayan de Oro City

Barangay Macabalan has a common way of celebrating fiesta but more lively ever. They celebrated it every 22th day of February. Macabalan is also know as "PIER" because the port was located in this area.

Macabalan Fiesta is celebrated with so many gatherings and competition such as DANCE COMPETITION, PAGEANT, SINGING CONTEST AND THE MOST AWAITED STREET DANCING COMPETITION which is me, myself had participated.
